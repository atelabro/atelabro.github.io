Privacy Policy

App: DuckOff — Developer: AL4Bro — Effective: March 11, 2025

This privacy policy explains how DuckOff handles information when you use the app. We are committed to keeping things simple and transparent.

Information We Do Not Collect

DuckOff does not require you to create an account. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or location.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

DuckOff displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to show you relevant ads. This may include your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android), approximate location, and usage data.

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > turn off Personalized Ads
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Delete advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads

For more information on how Google uses data, visit: policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob's own privacy policy is available at: support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

Children's Privacy

DuckOff is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address it.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into DuckOff is Google AdMob. No analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or social login services are used.

Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal data ourselves, we have nothing to retain or delete. Any data collected by AdMob is governed by Google's own retention policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if the app changes. Any updates will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
